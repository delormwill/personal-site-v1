Well, how did I get here?

I have always had a knack for working with technology. Simply put, I get it. So it made sense in 2021, with every one hitting the big reset button, for me to jump into programming as a career. As a teenager, I learned Java and SQL. More recently, I have gained a foundational knowledge of many front-end and back-end systems.

My previous professions have been in theatre design & production and the rock climbing industry. Both of these environments demand strong problem solving, communication and teamwork to achieve a successful product. I have honed these critical skills and have found myself adaptable to most-any situation that comes my way.

What gets me excited about web development is the enormous need for it in all industries. Curious as I am, I am looking forward to finding my skills used and challenged in an array of unlikely places.
